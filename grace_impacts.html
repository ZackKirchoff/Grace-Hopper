The lasting Impact of Grandma COBOL

Hopper's advancements toward better programming languages helped steer the way towards the computer science that we know today. With programming languages becoming higher level came with it the ability for people without degrees in mathmatics to write code that a computer could understand. This led the way for even more advanced languages to be developed and without those I would not even be able to make this webpage that you are viewing.

It would not be an exageration to say that the current state of computing is all thanks to people like grace hopper and their enormous efforts to iron out the "bugs" of early computing.
